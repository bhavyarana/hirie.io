Capabilities

Platform Features

Every feature built for real hiring teams — from candidate upload to final decision.

01
🤖

AI Resume Scoring

Mistral-powered scoring across 4 dimensions — Technical Skills, Experience, Education, Soft Skills — with per-job custom weights.

02
🎯

Custom Scoring Criteria

Set pass / review / fail score thresholds and dimension weights per job opening. AI adapts its model accordingly.

03
📤

Batch Resume Upload

Upload up to 100 resumes simultaneously (PDF & DOCX). BullMQ processes everything in the background.

04
⚡

Real-Time Pipeline

Live status updates while resumes are processed — queued → processing → scored. No polling, no waiting.

05
📊

Role-Based Analytics

Separate dashboard views per role. Admins see org-wide funnels. Managers track team output. TLs monitor pipelines.

06
🏢

Multi-Team Org Hierarchy

Full org structure: Admin → Manager → Team Lead → Recruiter. Every level has scoped, role-appropriate data access.

07
🔍

Talent Pool Search

Search all candidates across the org by skills, job title, and experience level. Build pipelines proactively.

08
📥

CSV Export & Reports

Export all candidate data, AI scores, matched skills, and pipeline status for any job with one click.

09
👥

Multi-Team Job Assignment

Jobs can be assigned to multiple teams simultaneously. Team Leads then assign individual recruiters to each job.

10
👑

User Management

Admins can create, edit, deactivate, and reassign roles for any user. Full org structure control in one place.

11
⚙️

Settings & Password Change

Users can update their own password. Admin-managed accounts with role & team configuration.

12
📞

AI Outbound Calling (Pipeline)

Automated candidate phone screening via Plivo + Deepgram + ElevenLabs + OpenAI. Transcripts stored and evaluated.

Role-Based Access

Who Can Do What

Each role gets exactly the tools and data they need — nothing more, nothing less.

👑
Admin
Full Platform Access
  • ✓User Management — Create, edit, deactivate any user; assign roles
  • ✓Org Structure — Create and manage all teams; assign managers
  • ✓All Jobs — View, create, and manage every job in the org
  • ✓All Candidates — Access every candidate across all jobs and teams
  • ✓Full Analytics — Org-wide funnels, pass/fail rates, team comparisons
  • ✓Resume Upload — Upload to any job in the org
  • ✓Scoring Config — Set AI criteria on any job
  • ✓CSV Export — Export data from any job
  • ✓Talent Pool — Search entire candidate database
👔
Manager
Team & Job Owner
  • ✓Job Creation — Create new jobs and configure scoring criteria
  • ✓Team Assignment — Assign teams and Team Leads to their jobs
  • ✓Their Jobs — Full visibility into all jobs they own or manage
  • ✓Team Analytics — Pass rates, recruiter leaderboard, team funnel
  • ✓Recruiter Output — Per-recruiter upload counts, scores, funnel stages
  • ✓Resume Upload — Upload to jobs they manage
  • ✓CSV Export — Export from their jobs
  • ✓Talent Pool — Search candidates in their scope
🧭
Team Lead
Team Pipeline Manager
  • ✓Recruiter Assignment — Assign recruiters to specific jobs
  • ✓Team Job Pipeline — All jobs assigned to their team
  • ✓Recruiter Tracking — Activity, uploads, and scores per recruiter
  • ✓Top Candidates — View top-ranked candidates for team's jobs
  • ✓Resume Upload — Upload to any job assigned to their team
  • ✓My Teams Page — Manage job ↔ recruiter assignments
  • ✓CSV Export — Export from assigned jobs
🧑‍💻
Recruiter
Candidate Uploader
  • ✓Resume Upload — Upload to jobs they are personally assigned to
  • ✓Candidate Management — View and manage candidates in assigned jobs
  • ✓Personal Analytics — Own funnel: upload count, pass rates, pipeline status
  • ✓Matched Skill Insights — AI-extracted skills vs. job requirements
  • ✓Pipeline Status — Live processing status of uploaded resumes
  • ✓CSV Export — Export from their assigned jobs

🔒 Data Scope Summary

Admin → Full platform — all orgs, teams, jobs, users, and candidates
Manager → Their jobs only + their assigned teams
Team Lead → Jobs assigned to their team + their team's recruiters
Recruiter → Only jobs they are personally assigned to
